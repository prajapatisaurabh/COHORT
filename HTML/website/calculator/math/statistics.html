How It Works

Based on the given set of numbers, the following formulas are used:

Mean

Mean = (Sum of all numbers) / (Total number of numbers)

Median

Median = Middle value when the numbers are arranged in ascending order. If even, average of two middle values.

Mode

Mode = The number that appears most frequently in the set.

Variance

Variance = Sum of squared differences from the mean / (Number of numbers)

Standard Deviation

Standard Deviation = Square root of Variance
