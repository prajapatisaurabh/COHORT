How It Works

Based on the selected shape, the formulae used are:

Circle

Area = π × radius²

Perimeter = 2 × π × radius

Square

Area = side²

Perimeter = 4 × side

Rectangle

Area = length × width

Perimeter = 2 × (length + width)

Triangle

Area = ½ × base × height

Perimeter = base + side1 + side2 (sides are given or calculated)
