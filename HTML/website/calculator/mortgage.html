How It Works

The mortgage payment is calculated using the amortization formula:

M = P [ i(1 + i)n ] / [ (1 + i)n - 1 ]

Where:

  • M = Monthly payment
  • P = Loan principal (Home Price - Down Payment)
  • i = Monthly interest rate (Annual Rate รท 12)
  • n = Total number of payments (Loan Term ร 12)
