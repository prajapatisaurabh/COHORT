Compound Interest Formula

Future value is calculated using compound interest formula:

A = P (1 + r/n)nt

Where:

  • A = Future value
  • P = Principal investment
  • r = Annual interest rate (decimal)
  • n = Compounding frequency per year
  • t = Investment duration in years
